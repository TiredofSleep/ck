## External Architecture — Memory Organism

The `ck_lm/memory/` organism architecture draws on these published works.
Each is credited inline in the relevant source files.
We are grateful and encourage direct engagement with their work.

| Authors | Paper | arXiv | What CK uses |
|---------|-------|-------|--------------|
| Zhang et al. | **RGMem: Renormalization Group Memory** | [2510.16392](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.16392) | Crystal promotion scoring — `promotion_score()` in `crystal_store.py` |
| Li et al. | **MAGMA: Multi-Graph Agentic Memory** | [2601.03236](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03236) | Dual-stream fast/slow write in `compression_loop.py` |
| Castillo et al. | **Sophia: Persistent Agent Framework** | [2512.18202](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.18202) | MetaCrystal + Adaptation Loop architecture |
| Wang et al. | **MemoryOS: Memory Operating System** | [2506.06326](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.06326) | Heat-score retention pruning — `prune_cold_crystals()`. Code: [BAI-LAB/MemoryOS](https://github.com/BAI-LAB/MemoryOS) |
| Chen et al. | **AtomMem: Learnable Atomic Memory Operations** | [2601.08323](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08323) | Atomic operation unit design; future novelty gate LoRA target |

If your work is contributing to CK and isn't listed — open an issue. We want to credit everyone.
