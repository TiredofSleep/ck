## §1 — Manuscript

**Path:** `manuscript/manuscript.tex` (amsart, ~14 pages)

**Title (revised):** *A Commutative Non-Associative 4-Algebra over F_5 with Rigid Idempotent Decomposition*
(Was: "Discrete Dirac on F_5⁴: Substrate Algebra of the 4-Core". Renamed per referee request — see §5 below.)

**Abstract.** We study a four-dimensional commutative non-associative algebra V over F_5 defined by the bilinear extension of an explicit 4×4 multiplication table. V has a remarkably rigid structure: exactly four idempotents (zero plus three non-zero), a 1-dim left annihilator ideal, eigenspace-dimension profiles (1, 3) and (2, 2) for two distinguished left-multiplication operators with trivial simultaneous eigenspace, 1-dim associator image, power-associativity, and automorphism group of order 40.

## §5 — Notes

**Family-Structure framing.** This paper sits within the broader research program on small finite commutative non-associative algebras with rigid structure. The closest published precedents:
- Drápal & Wanless (2021), *J. Combin. Theory Ser. A* **184**, 105510 — same domain (small finite commutative non-associative structures), opposite extremum (theirs maximally non-associative; ours has localized 1-dim associator image).
- Hall, Rehren, Shpectorov (2015), *J. Algebra* **421**, 394–424 — universal axial algebras and rigid-idempotent decomposition theme. V is conceptually proximal but not technically an axial algebra (lacks Frobenius form).

**PROVEN / COMPUTED / STRUCTURAL RHYME / OPEN — filled.**

- **PROVEN:** Theorem 3.1 (structural rigidity of V, full statement): nine claims (a)–(i) covering idempotents, annihilator, eigenspaces, commutation, associator localization, power-associativity, automorphism order. All proved inline in §3.
- **COMPUTED:** All claims verified by 14 algebraic checks + 15 unit tests in `tig_dirac.py`; <2 sec runtime.
- **STRUCTURAL RHYME:** Eigenspace profiles (1, 3) and (2, 2) and empty simultaneous eigenspace evoke Minkowski 1+3 / chirality 2+2 / V-A asymmetry of the relativistic Dirac equation. These are interpretive analogies, not theorems. The 32-cell binomial decomposition of V^⊗5 has same dimension count as SU(5) matter representation plus conjugate; this is a binomial-coefficient identity, not a representation-theoretic match.
- **OPEN:** Field-invariance for all primes $p \notin \{2, 5\}$ (verified up to 13 in companion); higher tensor powers and Bott-style periodicity; SU(5) action on V^⊗5; triality at n=4; comprehensive search for V's position in the broader landscape of similar algebras.

**Lens-ownership paragraph.** Works over F_5 with a specific 4-dim commutative non-associative algebra defined by an explicit multiplication table. The choice is foundational, not derived; it arose historically as the bilinear lift of a 4-element Z/10Z subset, but the math here doesn't depend on that origin.
