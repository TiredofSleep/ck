## 5. Thread 1 — Shell Attractor: Locked Results

**Lemma S (proved):** $\mathbb{E}[v_2(3T_*(n)+1) \mid v_2(3n+1) = s] \to 2$ as $k \to \infty$, for all fixed $s \geq 1$.

**Shell 3 exact characterization:** $v_2(3n+1) = 3$ iff $n \equiv 13 \pmod{16}$. **Exact** — verified computationally.

**Shell 3 role:** First corrective gate. One shell-3 step ($-1.415$ bits) cancels more than two shell-1 expansions ($+0.585$ each). Shell 3 is the mechanism by which the metastable 1↔2 cycle is periodically reset.
