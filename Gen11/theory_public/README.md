## What this record claims

- A set of theorems in $\mathbb{Z}/10\mathbb{Z}$ ring arithmetic, each with a complete algebraic proof.
- Structural bridge statements: precise specifications of what additional mechanism would be required to connect each proved result to an open mathematical problem.
- Formal negative results: proved obstructions to three candidate bridge approaches.

## What this record does not claim

- That any Millennium Prize Problem is solved.
- That structural similarity between the $\mathbb{Z}/10\mathbb{Z}$ invariants and classical invariants constitutes mathematical evidence beyond structural analogy.
- That any executable system implements these theorems in any applied context.

Every theorem is stated with a complete proof.
Every bridge statement names the missing mechanism precisely.
Every obstruction is a proved result, not an informal assessment.
