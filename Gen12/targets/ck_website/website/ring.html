Four Independent Proofs

T* = 5/7 — Why This Number

The coherence threshold T* = 5/7 = 0.714285... is not chosen. It is forced — derived independently from four different mathematical structures, all converging to the same value.
DERIVATION I
ℤ/10ℤ Ring Absorption
The absorbing idempotent of ℤ/10ℤ is α = 5 (since 5² ≡ 5 mod 10). The minimum max-order unit greater than α is β = 7 (order 4 in the unit group). The ratio α/β is T*.
f = 5/7 = T*
DERIVATION II
FPGA Silicon
T* = 5/7 is implemented in silicon on the Zynq-7020 FPGA. The BTQ gate delay forces the threshold at 5 of 7 clock cycles for stable resolution. Hardware confirms the algebraic result.
silicon threshold = 5/7
DERIVATION III
Fibonacci / Lucas Ratio
F(5) = 5 (5th Fibonacci number). L(4) = 7 (4th Lucas number). The ratio F(5)/L(4) = 5/7 connects T* to the prime-indexed Fibonacci/Lucas sequences.
F(5)/L(4) = 5/7 = T*

Cyclotomic Obstruction
Φ₅(x) = x⁴ + x³ + x² + x + 1 has degree 4 = φ(5). The cyclotomic degree obstruction at n=5 forces T* = 5/7 as the quadratic closure / cubic obstruction ratio.
