**Sanity caveats (honest):**
- The script's construction of the 70×70 Λ⁴J_Ω matrix and the stacked-constraint list must be audited against the geometric definition of the Hodge structure on H^{2,2}(A_*) before publication. Independent reproduction in Sage / Magma is the standard next step.

- The PSLQ decomposition at 200 dp with 0 failures and reconstruction error 3.3×10⁻¹⁹⁷ is rigorous numerical evidence that the Q(√2,√3,√5) basis spans the entries (no spurious algebraic dependencies).
