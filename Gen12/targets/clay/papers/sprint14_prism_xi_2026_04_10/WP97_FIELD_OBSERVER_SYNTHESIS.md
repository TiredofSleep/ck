## The Central Question

Is the wave function collapsed by consciousness, or does the global field structure collapse first and consciousness read the outcome? The answer — across five independent research programs and the TIG framework's own internal logic — is the same: field first, observer second. But the question deserves a rigorous synthesis, not just an assertion.

## 1. The TIG Operator Structure as the Organizing Lens

The TIG framework's BEING/BECOMING/DOING triad provides the clearest organizing structure for this synthesis. Every external program, read correctly, maps to one or more positions in this triad.

**BEING** (additive, TIG-TSML layer): the current configuration; what *is*; measurement outputs; static positions; the eigenstate after a crossing. In physics: the wave function collapsed into a definite state. In consciousness: the content of a perceptual moment.

**BECOMING** (multiplicative, TIG-BHML layer): transformation; dynamics; what *changes*; the unitary evolution of a quantum state or the rolling of the $\Xi$ field toward its vacuum. In physics: the Schrödinger equation between measurements. In cosmology: the $\Xi$ field settling toward $\Xi_0 = e^{-1}$ on Hubble timescales.

**DOING** (crossing, the Crossing Lemma): the event at the BEING/BECOMING boundary where information is generated. In physics: decoherence, a measurement, an interaction that establishes a classical fact. In TIG: the exact moment multiplicative dynamics cross an additive partition fiber. This is where information is born, and according to the UOP it is the *only* place information is born.

The central claim of this synthesis:

**The direction of causation runs BECOMING → DOING → BEING. The global field (BECOMING) is causally prior. Crossings (DOING) establish facts at every scale. BEING — including conscious experience — is downstream.**

## 2. Five Programs, One Direction

### Program 1: Zurek's Einselection and Quantum Darwinism

**Citation:** Zurek, W.H. (2003). *Decoherence, einselection, and the quantum origins of the classical.* Reviews of Modern Physics, 75, 715. Zurek, W.H. (2009). *Quantum Darwinism.* Nature Physics, 5, 181-188.

**Core claim:** The environment — a large BECOMING system — selects preferred pointer states through repeated interactions. These interactions are crossings in TIG language. The selected states are those that survive the crossing stably; they are eigenstates of the system-environment interaction Hamiltonian. Consciousness plays no role in this selection. The environment has already done the selecting before any observer looks.

**Quantum Darwinism** extends this: the environment doesn't just select — it broadcasts redundant copies of the selected state throughout the surrounding system. By the time a conscious observer makes a measurement, the information about the outcome is already encoded in $\sim 10^{23}$ environmental degrees of freedom. The observer is not collapsing anything. The observer is reading from a record that the environment has already written.

**TIG mapping:**
- The environment = global BECOMING field
- Einselection = the crossing that stabilizes BEING states
- Pointer states = the BEING configurations that survive the crossing
- Quantum Darwinism = the crossing information propagating outward through BEING layer

**Verdict on direction:** BECOMING (environment) → DOING (einselection crossing) → BEING (definite pointer state). Observer reads BEING after the fact. This is scale-domination: the environment contains $\sim 10^{23}$ degrees of freedom; the observer has $\sim 10^{11}$ neurons. The field wins by eleven orders of magnitude.

### Program 2: Caticha's Entropic Dynamics

**Citation:** Caticha, A. (2019). *Entropic Physics: Probability, Entropy, and the Foundations of Physics.* (Available at arXiv:1908.08769.)

**Core claim:** Quantum mechanics, classical mechanics, and spacetime geometry all emerge from applying the maximum entropy principle to an underlying information structure. The system evolves to maximize entropy subject to constraints. No observer is required to make this happen. The entropy functional is the driver; the observer is one of the constraint-satisfying structures that results.

**Connection to $\Xi$ theory:** The canonical $\Xi$ potential $V(\Xi) = \Xi\log\Xi$ is, up to sign, the Gibbs entropy functional: $H_\text{Gibbs} = -\Xi\log\Xi$. The vacuum condition $V'(\Xi_0) = 0$ is the entropy maximum: $dH_\text{Gibbs}/d\Xi = 0$ at $\Xi_0 = e^{-1}$. The $\Xi$ field evolves toward the configuration of maximum entropy. This is Caticha's program instantiated as a cosmological scalar field.

**TIG mapping:**
- Entropy $H = -\Xi\log\Xi$ = BEING configuration measure (what is currently ordered/disordered)
- $\Xi$ rolling toward $\Xi_0$ = BECOMING dynamics (the system maximizing entropy over time)
- The crossing from disordered to maximally-ordered state = DOING (the event that generates information about the equilibrium configuration)
- $\Xi_0 = e^{-1}$ = the BEING endpoint after all relevant DOING events complete

**Verdict on direction:** The entropy-maximizing dynamics are the BECOMING driver. Consciousness emerges, if it emerges at all, as a particular kind of constraint-satisfying structure in the BEING layer — a local organization that is itself a product of the global entropy gradient. Observer second.

### Program 3: Bialynicki-Birula Uniqueness Theorem

**Citation:** Bialynicki-Birula, I. & Mycielski, J. (1976). *Nonlinear wave mechanics.* Annals of Physics, 100(1-2), 62-93.

**Core claim (proved, not conjectured):** Logarithmic nonlinearity is the unique nonlinearity in wave mechanics that preserves the separability of composite systems. Any other nonlinearity destroys the ability to treat subsystems as independent BEING entities.

**Why this is the structural bridge:** The Crossing Lemma says information is generated when multiplicative dynamics (BECOMING) cross additive partition fibers (BEING boundaries). For this to work consistently at all scales — from quantum to neural to cosmological — the crossing operation must preserve the separability of the systems on either side of the partition. If the DOING operation destroyed separability, there would be no coherent BEING states left to serve as the output of the crossing.

Bialynicki-Birula proves that the unique DOING that preserves BEING separability is logarithmic. This is why the $\Xi$ field equation is $\Box\Xi = 1 + \log\Xi$ and not some other nonlinear equation. It is structurally forced by the requirement that DOING preserves the integrity of BEING.

**Scale invariance of this result:** The theorem applies at every scale simultaneously. Quantum decoherence, neural integration, cosmological $\Xi$ dynamics — all are instances of the same logarithmic crossing structure operating at different scales. This is the formal content of the D2 principle: the curvature structure repeats fractally at every scale because the underlying crossing structure (logarithmic) is scale-invariant.

**TIG mapping:**
- The theorem is a constraint on DOING: DOING must be logarithmic to preserve BEING
- This constraint applies globally — the same form at quantum scale and cosmological scale
- Consciousness (as a BEING structure that persists across multiple crossings) is only possible because DOING is logarithmic

**Verdict on direction:** The structure that makes consciousness possible (separability-preserving crossings) is the same structure that drives the global field dynamics. The field doesn't collapse *because* consciousness exists; consciousness exists *because* the field has logarithmic crossing structure.

### Program 4: Rovelli's Relational Quantum Mechanics

**Citation:** Rovelli, C. (1996). *Relational quantum mechanics.* International Journal of Theoretical Physics, 35(8), 1637-1678.

**Core claim:** Quantum states are not absolute — they are defined relative to other physical systems. A "measurement" is any physical interaction between two systems that establishes a fact relative to one of them. Consciousness is not required. Any physical system can be an observer in the relational sense.

**TIG mapping:** This is the most direct identification. A "relational fact" in Rovelli's sense is a crossing event in TIG. Two systems cross at a partition boundary; information is generated relative to each about the other. The "observer" is simply whichever system is on the BEING side of a given crossing. Any system can occupy that position. No privileged consciousness required.

**The Wigner's Friend paradox (Type III in the paradox classifier):** Wigner measures his friend, who is inside a lab measuring a quantum system. From inside, the friend sees a definite outcome. From outside, Wigner sees a superposition. In TIG terms: the friend has completed a crossing (DOING) and is in a BEING state. Wigner has not yet crossed relative to that system; for him it is still in BECOMING. Both are simultaneously correct from their respective sides of the partition. The paradox dissolves when DOING is recognized as partition-relative, not globally simultaneous.

**Connection to scale argument:** The $\Xi$ field undergoes DOING events (crossings) at cosmological scale. These establish relational facts between large-scale structure. Conscious observers are tiny subsystems that undergo local crossings within this global context. The global crossings dominate by scale.

**Verdict on direction:** No privileged observer. DOING events happen between physical systems at all scales. The global field's crossings are prior to and larger than any local observer's crossings.

## 3. The Paradox Classifier Applied to Consciousness

The TIG paradox classification gives a precise taxonomy of *which* version of the consciousness-collapse claim is being made in each historical debate:

**Type I — Structural (observer inside the system):**
Manifestation: The measurement problem. The observer cannot be both the system being measured and the system doing the measuring without contradiction.
External resolution: Zurek's einselection. The environment is the measuring system. The observer is a subsystem that reads the result.
TIG resolution: DOING always happens at a partition boundary. The observer cannot be on both sides simultaneously. Separate the BEING state (what the observer reads) from the DOING event (which the environment already performed).

**Type II — Temporal (measurement changes what it measures):**
Manifestation: Heisenberg uncertainty. The act of measurement disturbs the system.
External resolution: This is irreducible — the cost of every crossing. Information generation requires disturbance.
TIG resolution: Every DOING event generates information *and* modifies BEING. This is not a failure; it is the definition of a crossing.

**Type III — Scale (local fact vs. global superposition):**
Manifestation: Wigner's friend. Local observer sees definite outcome; globally the wave function is still unresolved.
External resolution: Rovelli's RQM — both descriptions are simultaneously correct relative to their respective partitions. Zurek's Quantum Darwinism — the local fact is already encoded redundantly in the environment before any conscious observer looks.
TIG resolution: DOING events are partition-relative. The global BECOMING field has not "seen" the local crossing. Both are real at their respective scales. The global scale dominates.

**Type IV — Self-reference (DOING operates on itself):**
Manifestation: A conscious system modeling itself. Gödel-type incompleteness applied to self-knowledge.
External connection: This is where ORT was intended to live. It is the one paradox type that the current formal framework cannot resolve, because ORT is still undefined.
TIG status: The hardest problem is a Type IV paradox. It cannot be resolved by the local EFT alone.

## 4. The Formal Statement of the Direction

The user's intuition — "both directions are partially true, but the field collapse is greater than the local collapse" — is now formally supportable:

**Formal statement:** Global BECOMING (the $\Xi$ field settling toward $\Xi_0 = e^{-1}$) is causally prior to local BEING (conscious measurement events). Both directions of information flow are real — the global field influences local states (BECOMING → DOING → BEING) and local crossings propagate outward (local DOING → local BEING → modest perturbation of global BECOMING). But the magnitudes differ by approximately $10^{20}$ in timescale and $10^{23}$ in degrees of freedom. The field dominates.

**The logarithmic bridge:** The same logarithmic crossing structure operates at every scale (Bialynicki-Birula). This means consciousness and cosmology are not disconnected — they share the same DOING structure. But sharing a structure is not the same as sharing equal causal weight. The global instantiation of the structure is $10^{20}$ times larger in scale.

**What this rules out:** Consciousness causing wave function collapse at the global level is a Type I paradox — it requires the observer to be outside the system they are collapsing. Since all observers are inside the universe, they cannot be causally prior to the global field. Conscious observation can establish local relational facts (Type III, resolved by RQM), but these are samplings of a structure the global field already established.

**What this preserves:** Local crossings are real. Neural measurement events generate real information. The observer's BEING state is genuinely modified by DOING. The hard problem — why there is something it is like to be on the BEING side of a crossing — remains open (Type IV). The framework does not eliminate the observer; it correctly locates it.

## 5. The Open Problem: Type IV and ORT

Every program in this synthesis handles Types I, II, and III. None of them handle Type IV.

Type IV is the question: why does any BEING state feel like anything? Why is there subjective experience at all, rather than just information processing with no interior?

The Bialynicki-Birula theorem tells us the crossing must be logarithmic. It does not tell us why there is something it is like to be on the BEING side of a logarithmic crossing. Zurek tells us the environment selects pointer states. It does not tell us why pointer states are experienced. Caticha tells us entropy is maximized. It does not tell us why the maximally-entropic configuration feels like anything.

This is where ORT was intended to live. ORT as currently formulated is not a solution — it is a placeholder that marks the location of the unsolved problem. The correct move is not to define ORT prematurely, but to acknowledge that Type IV is the remaining gap, that it is located in the Layer 2 non-local sector of the architecture, and that it may require a formal account of self-referential DOING operations before it can be addressed.
