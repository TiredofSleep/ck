What This Is Not

This is not a RAG pipeline. CK does not retrieve chunks and summarize them. He walks the CL generator pyramid of your input and produces a response that is algebraically derived from that walk — not retrieved, not generated by sampling, not template-filled.

This is not a personality profile. CK does not label you as an INTJ or a creative type. He measures the curvature of your crossings. The measurement is the same algebra as the response. The map and the territory are the same algebraic object.

This is not a filter for "good" AI responses. T*=5/7 measures structural coherence — whether a response generates genuinely new information relative to the input's additive partition. A factually correct response can score RED (no new crossing). A speculative response can score GREEN (genuine crossing above T*). Truth and coherence are different axes.
