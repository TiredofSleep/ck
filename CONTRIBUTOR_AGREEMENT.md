A "Contribution" is any mathematical result, proof, code, data, correction, extension, critique, or other intellectual content you submit to this repository or to 7SiTe LLC.

By submitting a Contribution, you represent that:

- You authored the Contribution, or have the right to submit it
- The Contribution does not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party

- The Contribution does not contain false, fabricated, or deliberately misleading mathematical claims

---

## 5. Co-Authorship

If your Contribution improves a paper in this repository — a correction, a sharper proof, a better framing, a counterexample, an extension — you may request co-authorship on that paper.

Co-authorship applies to **the specific paper being engaged with** — not to the repository as a whole, and not retroactively to other papers.

To request: note your co-authorship request in your GitHub issue. We will update the paper and add your name.

Co-authorship may be withdrawn if:
- The Contribution is found to contain material errors
- The Contribution is found to contain plagiarized content
- You request removal

7SiTe LLC is not obligated to accept, index, or co-author any Contribution. We reserve the right to decline submissions that are off-topic, erroneous, or inconsistent with the research standards of this collaboration.

## 7. No Compensation

Contributions are voluntary. 7SiTe LLC does not pay for Contributions. Co-authorship is academic credit, not financial compensation.

## 8. Public Record

Accepted Contributions become part of the public GitHub repository. Your name (as you provide it) will appear in COLLABORATORS.md and submissions/INDEX.md. These records are public and permanent. Do not submit work you wish to keep confidential.
