what this looks like in physics

interpretive — not derived

The flag tangent has three equivalent root-pair classes related by Weyl ℤ₃ rotation — a structural symmetry that physical states can break. Generation structure works this way: three replicas of a fermion species, related by an underlying ℤ₃, with mass terms (or Yukawa couplings) breaking the symmetry to distinguish them.

The substrate produces the same shape (3 pairs with ℤ₃ rotation) at the geometric level (σ² action on the σ-cycle), and breaks it at the algebraic level (TSML BUMP cells distinguishing P3). If this analogy carries weight, P3 = {COUNTER, CHAOS} is the substrate's distinguished generation, and the BUMP positions encode something like a generation-distinguishing structure in nascent form.

what this is not

honesty fence

Shape match, not structural isomorphism. The σ-cycle and the flag tangent share their ℤ₃-rotational architecture, but the substrate's pair-additivity (Minkowski sum mod 10) does not reproduce the root-sum relation α₁ + α₂. The Killing form on root planes has not been derived from any natural bilinear operation on substrate pairs.

What's been verified: (1) σ² acts as ℤ₃ rotation on three σ³-pairs; (2) TSML breaks this ℤ₃ asymmetrically, distinguishing P3. What's not done: a rigorous map from substrate operators to A₂ root vectors, or a derivation of the Killing form from substrate composition.

The right way to read this: the substrate has the geometric scaffold of A₂; the algebra picks out a preferred direction. Whether that preferred direction matches anything physical is the next question.
