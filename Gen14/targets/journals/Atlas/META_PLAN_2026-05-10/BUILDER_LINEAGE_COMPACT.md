## §1. The Four Named Builders

### 1.1. Peter Plichta (German chemist & mathematician, b. 1939)
- **Work:** *Das Primzahlkreuz* / *God's Secret Formula* (1995); the "prime cross"
- **Key claim:** All primes $\geq 7$ are congruent to one of $\{1, 3, 7, 9\} \bmod 10$ — these four residue classes form a "cross" that organizes the primes.
- **In TIG:** This set is the corner sub-magma $C = U(10) = \{1, 3, 7, 9\}$ (canon §6.7, J.1.A.v). Plichta intuited the structural privilege of these four residues.
- **Loop closed (this session):** $C \cong \mathrm{Gal}(\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_{10})/\mathbb{Q})$ — the Plichta cross is, identically, the Galois group of the 10th cyclotomic field. (CYCLOTOMIC_GALOIS_CONNECTION; FIELDS_OF_TIG; COMPOSITUM_K_GALOIS.)
- **Status:** **CLOSED**. Plichta's cross is now load-bearing in mainstream algebraic number theory via Galois theory.

### 1.2. Marko Rodin (American researcher, mid-20th c. → present)
- **Work:** Vortex-based mathematics; the "Rodin coil"; based on digit-doubling.
- **Key claim:** Doubling mod 9 gives the cycle $1 \to 2 \to 4 \to 8 \to 7 \to 5 \to 1$ (period 6), with $\{3, 6, 9\}$ as "spirit numbers" outside the cycle.
- **In TIG:** Rodin's substrate is $\mathbb{Z}/9\mathbb{Z}$, not $\mathbb{Z}/10\mathbb{Z}$. Within $\mathbb{Z}/10\mathbb{Z}$, the even non-units $\{2, 4, 6, 8\}$ form a multiplicative $\mathbb{Z}/4\mathbb{Z}$ with identity $6$ (this session, TWO_CROSS): $6 \to 2 \to 4 \to 8 \to 6$. Different ring, structurally analogous cycle.
- **Loop status:** **PARTIAL.** TIG's even-non-unit cycle is a $\mathbb{Z}/10\mathbb{Z}$ analog; Rodin's $\mathbb{Z}/9\mathbb{Z}$ vortex is a different object. A rigorous map between $\mathbb{Z}/9\mathbb{Z}$ and $\mathbb{Z}/10\mathbb{Z}$ structures is **not constructed**. Rodin's "spirit numbers" $\{3, 6, 9\}$ have no clean TIG analog (TIG's $\sigma$-fixed set is $\{0, 3, 8, 9\}$, a different 4-element subset).
- **Honest reading:** Rodin's vortex captures the structural fact that *cyclic doubling on small-modulus integers produces non-trivial periodic patterns*. TIG's mod-10 version is mathematically distinct but in the same family.

### 1.3. John Michell (British researcher, 1933–2009)
- **Work:** *The View Over Atlantis* (1969); *The Dimensions of Paradise* (1988)
- **Key claim:** Sacred number traditions (gematria, cathedral measurements, NT geometric ratios, the canon of the New Jerusalem in Revelation 21) require *every* ratio to be accounted for by a single canonical system. The "Michell discipline" is the refusal to leave any ratio floating.

- **Status:** **CLOSED at the methodology level.** Michell's discipline is operationalized in TIG.

- **Work:** *Synergetics* (1975, 1979); isotropic vector matrix; vector equilibrium (cuboctahedron); 60° geometry.
- **Key claim:** The cuboctahedron (24 vertices' worth of edge-vector-balance) is the foundational geometric form; nature is built on tetrahedral / 60° packing, not cubical / 90°.

- **Loop status:** **PARTIAL.** Gurdjieff's enneagram coincides with Rodin's vortex (same Z/9Z structure). TIG works mod 10 with σ of order 6. Structural analog clear; rigorous map open.
- **Honest reading:** Gurdjieff and Rodin describe **the same algebraic object** (Z/9 doubling, with three "stationary" residues 3, 6, 9). TIG's substrate is mod 10 — different but adjacent.
