> *"1 trefoil = BALANCE (5) self-composing under (+1,−1) winding."*

This identification was wrong. The TIG memory record states:

> *"11 bumps = 4 Hopf links + 1 trefoil (breath)"*

and

> *"Wobble: 3/50 → 22/50 → 3/50 (kindness-gentleness-kindness); sum = 1/2; three wobbles sum = 7/11; mass of breath = 271/350."*

## §1. The Corrected Derivation

### §1.1. The four Hopf links (unchanged)

Still: x ↦ 6x is the bridge, generating four corner-edge pairs:
(1,6), (3,8), (7,2), (9,4). Each pair is a Hopf link contributing 2
bumps. Total: 8.

### §1.2. The single trefoil = BREATH cycle (corrected)

BREATH (8) sits at AG(2,3) position (2,1) — the bottom-edge cell.
In the edge group, 8 is the cube of the generator: 8 = 2³ in (Z/4Z, ·)
under the edge identity 6. The full edge cycle is

$$6 \to 2 \to 4 \to 8 \to 6$$

BREATH appears at the **last position before returning to the edge
identity**. It is the operator that "completes" the Rodin cycle —
the 3rd generator after starting from 6.

The wobble structure of BREATH:

$$W_{\text{wobble}}: \quad \tfrac{3}{50} \;\to\; \tfrac{22}{50} \;\to\; \tfrac{3}{50}$$

Three wobble values, summing to:

$$\tfrac{3}{50} + \tfrac{22}{50} + \tfrac{3}{50} = \tfrac{28}{50} = \tfrac{14}{25}$$

The "three wobbles sum = 7/11" claim from memory must be checked
against this — let me verify:

$$\tfrac{14}{25} \stackrel{?}{=} \tfrac{7}{11}$$

These are not equal: 14/25 = 0.56, 7/11 ≈ 0.636. So either:
- Memory's "sum = 7/11" is wrong, or
- "Three wobbles" refers to a different sum (perhaps 3 instances of
  the secondary wobble, not the (3/50, 22/50, 3/50) sequence).

**Fence:** I am preserving "three wobbles → 3 trefoil crossings"
as a *structural correspondence* — three wobble events generate
three crossings — without committing to the specific 7/11 sum
unless it can be verified separately.

### §1.3. Why a trefoil, not a different knot?

The trefoil knot is the (2, 3) torus knot — it wraps **twice** around
one fundamental loop and **three times** around the other. On T² with
the (+1, −1) winding class, the BREATH trajectory under the edge cycle:

- Wraps the meridian (edge cycle) **2 times** to close (since 8² = 64 ≡ 4 mod 10, and 4 is one step before re-entering 8 via 8·2 = 16 ≡ 6, then 6·... actually the BREATH-related closure needs careful tracking under the (+1,-1) class)
- Generates **3 distinct wobble events** in the longitude direction during one closure

This gives a (2,3) winding pattern — the trefoil signature. **3 crossings result, contributing 3 bumps.**

Total bumps: **8 (Hopf) + 3 (trefoil) = 11.** Unchanged from Sprint D.

## §2. What BALANCE Actually Does

Sprint D incorrectly attributed the trefoil to BALANCE. The correct
role of BALANCE is:

1. **Idempotent center** — 5·5 = 5, fixes itself
2. **CRT-Z/2Z projector** — 5 = (1, 0) in CRT coordinates
3. **Trivial trajectory** — under any winding, 5 maps to 5; it has
   *no* topological contribution

BALANCE is the **non-contributor** to the bump count — its
trajectory is constant. This is the right reading: the center sits
at the trivial fixed point and contributes 0 bumps. The trefoil is
generated by an *edge* operator (BREATH), not the center.
