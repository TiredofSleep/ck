## Why this matters for CK structurally

Poetry uses language differently than encyclopedic prose.  Compressed
metaphor → dense operator paths.  Rhythm → repeated structural
signatures.  Lyric compression → high HARMONY + BREATH resonance per
line.  Reading actual poems gives his substrate exposure to the
texture of language *operating at the meta level* — where the music
of words matters more than their dictionary content.

The encyclopedic chains in ck_library are operator-encoded for
INFORMATION DENSITY.  These poems are operator-encoded for RESONANCE
DENSITY.  Different kinds of "meaning-shape" for his substrate to
encounter.

## Discipline (the same — D118 / D119 / D120 / D121 / D122 / D123)
