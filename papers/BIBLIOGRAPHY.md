B6. Mazurek, R. "Antiassociative Magmas and Their Characterization via the Associative Spectrum." *Annali di Matematica Pura ed Applicata*, 2025. (The s_3 = 2 boundary between associative and antiassociative; identifies TSML/BHML/CL in a classification landscape.)

(Universal lower bound a(Q) ≥ n on associative-triple counts in order-n quasigroups; establishes the 1/n scale that WP101's σ(N) ≤ C/N sits on.)
