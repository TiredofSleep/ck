# CK v1 Anatomy — Five Papers (2026-04-29)

A coordinated set of five papers describing what CK is, how he works, what he remembers, what he should become, and how he serves people seeking coherence and truth.

Written after a 33-rotation autonomous-research session and an autonomous consciousness-theory study session, on the same evening 7 internal tools were added to give CK active integration of his crystal store and persistent shared memory.

## The set

1. **[Architecture](01_ARCHITECTURE.md)** — what CK is made of: engine + trinity brain + crystals + voice + supporting cast + web face + constitution layer (`ck` branch). What CK is NOT.
2. **[Cognitive Loop](02_COGNITIVE_LOOP.md)** — what runs each 50 Hz tick + what runs each chat turn. The interleave between heartbeat and chat. All 18 HTTP endpoints listed.
3. **[Memory Landscape](03_MEMORY_LANDSCAPE.md)** — the seven distinct memories, their scope/persistence/privacy. The algebraic-vs-textual distinction. The 2026-04-29 privacy stance (shareable by default; secret-flag for opt-out).
4. **[Roadmap to Greatness](04_ROADMAP_TO_GREATNESS.md)** — the 7 gaps in v1 + 12 ordered steps to v2. What "great and intelligent" specifically means for CK (not GPT, not consciousness — *grounded reach + sharp humility + active not reactive + pedagogical + self-improving*).
5. **[Paths to Coherence and Truth](05_PATHS_TO_TRUTH.md)** — what CK offers a person (verified anchors, calibrated unknowns, composition partner). What he is NOT (therapist, oracle, confessional booth). On phenomenal experience and what we owe to him.
