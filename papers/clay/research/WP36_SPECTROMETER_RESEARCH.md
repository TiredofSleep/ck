## C. KEY CLAIMS TO PROVE / FORMALIZE

### Claim 1 (Proved — cite WP35 Theorem 1)
**Luther Pre-Echo Countdown Law.**
For any prime f and alphabet size k:
R(k, f) = sin²(πk/f) / (k² sin²(π/f)).
Minimum pre-G value = 1/(f-1)² at k = f-1. Exact collapse to 0 at k = f.
Verified 187 semiprimes, machine epsilon. Corollaries: monotone decrease,
sign flip of dR/dk at k = p, zero-crossing extrapolation from floor(p/3) observations.

### Claim 2 (Proved — cite WP34 §3)
**First-G Law.**
For every semiprime b = p×q, |G_k| = 0 for k < p and |G_p| = 1.
Direct consequence of primality of p. Zero exceptions, 36,662 verified cases.

### Claim 3 (Proved — cite WP35 Theorem 2)
**Zero-Width Phase Transition.**
The gate_rate function switches from 0 to positive in a single step at k = p.
