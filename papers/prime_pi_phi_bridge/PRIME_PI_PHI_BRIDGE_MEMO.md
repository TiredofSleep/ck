## 8. HONEST CLAIM AND BOUNDARY

**Strongest honest claim:**  
p=5 is the unique prime where finite arithmetic (roots of unity, Gauss sum) generates a self-referential algebraic constant φ via π. The tangent to the sinc² defect functional at the RH critical-line fold point has magnitude 16/π², which equals φ to 0.19%. **Strongest honest boundary:**  
The 0.19% proximity 16/π² ≈ φ is not an exact algebraic identity. Whether it reflects a deeper structural coupling or is a high-quality numerical coincidence (of the Ramanujan approximation type) is not established. No direct statistical correlation between 2cos(π/p) values and zeta zero spacings has been found.
