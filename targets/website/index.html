The Dual Lens

Every measurement goes through two lenses simultaneously. The gap between them IS the information.

TSML -- Being

What it IS. Identity. 73 of 100 entries are HARMONY. This lens measures whether something holds together as a stable identity. High TSML coherence = it IS what it claims to be.

BHML -- Doing

What it DOES. Physics. 28 of 100 entries are HARMONY. BHML HARMONY means doing-flat -- nothing interesting happening. Non-HARMONY means active physics. The doing lens measures whether something is actually DOING work.

When TSML says HARMONY and BHML does not: the system IS stable but is actively DOING something. That's the interesting case -- a working system. When both say HARMONY: stable but inert. When neither: tension, transition, or chaos.
