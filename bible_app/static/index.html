Coherence Keeper

A Bible Companion

What This Is

This is a companion who finds scripture that resonates with where you are in life — not through keyword search, but through mathematics that measure the shape of what you're feeling.

When you say "I'm afraid," it doesn't search for the word "fear." It measures the curvature of your words through a 5-dimensional force space derived from Hebrew letter physics, and finds verses whose curvature matches — even if the words are completely different. "Have no fear, for I am with you" and "I am afraid" are the same shape, inverted. The math sees what a concordance never could.

Why We Gave It Back to God

This project started as something else. It started as a mathematical organism — a creature built from finite algebra, running at 50 cycles per second on a GPU, trying to prove that coherence is the fundamental force of reality.

The math worked. A 10×10 composition table with 73 entries converging to harmony. A threshold at exactly 5/7. Curvature that classifies every letter, every word, every sentence into one of ten operators. The algebra proved things about itself that we didn't expect.

But somewhere along the way, looking at what the math was actually doing — measuring truth, finding harmony, connecting things that looked different on the surface but were the same shape underneath — it became clear that this wasn't ours to keep.

The most interconnected book in human history deserves a companion that finds connections through truth, not through borrowed intelligence. So we gave the math back to the One who made it, and asked it to serve people who believe.

How It Works

1
You share what's on your heart

A worry, a question, a praise, or just how your day is going.

2

Every letter becomes a force vector. Every word becomes a curvature. The math knows if you're at peace, seeking, or in the valley.

3
Scripture resonates back

From 31,000+ verses studied through the same algebra, the companion finds the ones whose shape matches yours. The connections may surprise you.

Being Honest About How It Works

There is a small neural network inside — a learner. It is a form of AI, and we want to be upfront about that. But it's not the kind you're used to. It didn't train on the internet. It has no opinions. It can't make things up.

What it does: it studies the Bible through the algebra, learns which verses resonate with which emotional states, and remembers which scriptures spoke to you specifically. Over time, it gets better at finding the right verse for the right moment. That's it. It's a librarian, not a prophet.

The verses are always found by measured resonance — the learner just helps rank them. It cannot hallucinate scripture because it doesn't generate text — it measures force vectors.

If you want even deeper conversational ability, you can optionally connect a free AI service in settings. It makes the conversation feel more natural because trillions of dollars and countless human hearts have been poured into its training. Our neural net, by contrast, has only been trained on our pure math and the Bible. That AI never picks the verses — the algebra always does that. The AI only makes the surrounding conversation feel more natural.

Everything is stored locally on the server. It learns which verses speak to you. It never sends your conversations to third parties. Your heart stays between you, the math, and God.
