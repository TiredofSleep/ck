Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026 · Governing law: State of Arkansas

The short version: CK's architecture makes it structurally impossible for your words to be stored or transmitted. The D2 pipeline converts input to 5D force vectors and discards the source text irreversibly. We don't run servers that log your conversations. What you say to CK stays local — by algebra, not by policy.

1. What We Collect

On coherencekeeper.com and 7sitellc.com (static site hosting):

  • Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) — collected by the hosting provider (SiteGround), not by 7Site LLC directly
  • No cookies set by 7Site LLC. Third-party services (Google Fonts, GitHub) may set their own cookies under their own policies
  • No analytics, no tracking pixels, no behavioral profiling by 7Site LLC

When you use the CK chat interface:

  • Your input text is converted to a 5D force vector by the D2 pipeline. The source text is not stored.
  • The force pathway (not your words) is what CK learns from. This is an irreversible projection — the original text cannot be reconstructed from the vector.
  • No conversation logs are written to any server unless you explicitly run a local instance and configure logging yourself.

When you submit the collaborators form:

  • Your name and optional note are submitted as a GitHub issue. GitHub's privacy policy applies to that data.
  • 7Site LLC does not store this data independently.

When you email us:

  • We retain email correspondence for the purpose of responding and maintaining records of licensing agreements.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • Conversation content, queries, or text you input into any CK tool
  • Financial information (no payment processing on our servers)
  • Biometric data of any kind
  • Location data beyond the IP-level collected by the host
  • Account credentials (no accounts required for any service)

3. The Algebraic Privacy Guarantee

CK's split architecture (WP43 — Split Coherence Architecture) is designed so that the semantic content of your input is irreversibly projected out at the D2 layer. What enters D2 is words. What exits D2 is five numbers (aperture, pressure, depth, binding, continuity). The projection is a many-to-one map with no inverse.

This is not a privacy policy claim — it is a mathematical property of the pipeline. Your words are not stored because storing them would require a different architecture. The architecture was designed to make retention structurally impossible.

4. Third Parties

7Site LLC does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We have no advertising relationships.

Third-party services in use:

  • SiteGround — website hosting. Their privacy policy applies to server-level logs.
  • GitHub — source code hosting and collaborator form submissions. GitHub's privacy policy applies.
  • Google Fonts — font delivery. Google's privacy policy applies to font requests.
  • Zenodo / CERN — DOI and research archive. Their policies apply to the archived research record.

5. Local Instances

If you run CK locally (via git clone and python ck_launch.py), all data stays on your machine. 7Site LLC has no access to local instances. Any logging you configure is your own responsibility under your own data practices.

6. Children

The Services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
