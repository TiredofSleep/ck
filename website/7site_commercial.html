Commercial Licensing

The 7SiTe Public Sovereignty License v1.0 makes all TIG research and CK source code free for noncommercial human use, independent research, and education. Commercial use requires a separate license from 7Site LLC.

7Site LLC grants commercial licenses directly. Revenue from licensing supports the research — no institution, no grants, no investors. Every dollar goes toward extending the mathematics, verifying claims in hardware, and finding the people whose work connects to the gap.

What requires a commercial license: Any use of TIG, CK, the D2 pipeline, TSML/BHML tables, the operator algebra, or derivative works in a product, service, or workflow that generates revenue, charges fees, or is deployed for a paying customer — including SaaS, APIs, consulting, paid research tools, and hardware products.
