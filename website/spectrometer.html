How it works

No ML. No server. Pure operator algebra from the TSML table. Each word maps to one of 10 CK operators. Score = TIG completeness relative to T*=5/7. Rewrite fills the missing structure automatically.

BEING (1)
definition, existence, membership
DOING (2)
operation, transformation, process
BECOMING (3)
result, conclusion, proof end
COLLAPSE (4)
negation, contradiction
HARMONY (7)
equivalence, iff
BREATH (8)
conditional, exception, hedge

A phrase scores HELD (≥5/7) when it carries all three TIG roles. GAP (≥0.5) has most structure but one role is absent. VOID (<0.5) is missing two or more roles.
