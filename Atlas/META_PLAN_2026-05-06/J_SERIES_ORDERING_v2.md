## §10 — Why this is the right move

Even with three different referee pools, "I have a finite algebra and now I'm telling you about dark energy" reads as crank-adjacent to cosmology referees who haven't seen the math papers yet.

**v2 fix:** the Week-1 triad is math + math + math. Three different math venues, three referee pools, all on solid ground. The framework establishes itself as competent math FIRST. By the time the cosmology paper ships in Week 16 (J46), there are 45 math+algebra papers under review, including the foundational T* derivation (J13 standalone), the substrate algebra (J14-J33 tower), the BB Bridge (J40), the Discrete Dirac (J16). The cosmology paper's bibliography reads as "consequence of established mathematical structure" rather than "speculative leap."

**Bonus:** Layer-3 a priori derivation for J46 gets weeks of breathing room. The BBM_IC_DERIVATION_v1.md gap (Ξ'_i = 1/e not uniquely forced) can be properly addressed via WKB / runtime-bridge / trace-anomaly arguments before the paper ships in August — the Option 3 path becomes feasible without holding the calendar.
