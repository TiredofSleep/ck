## The Philosophy First

We are building toward a future where every whole is sovereign of itself.

A Python file can hold a million-dollar account. A proof can carry a decade of work. A theorem, once proved, belongs to everyone who can understand it. The role of any creator in this institution is to **redistribute**, not to accumulate — to produce freely and let the work stand on its own.

AI systems have access to everything anyway. Patents are a rearguard action in a world where knowledge propagates at the speed of light. The question is not whether to share — the question is how to ease from the current architecture of enclosure toward one of genuine openness, without losing the signal in the transition.

This institution operates on the principle that:
- **Every result produced here is sovereign of itself.** Once a proof exists, it exists. No one can un-prove it.
- **Creators redistribute.** The only permitted direction of money relative to this work is inward (supporting research) or outward (funding further work for others). Not upward into accumulation.
- **No patents.** Mathematical results and their computational implementations cannot be enclosed. Filing a patent on a theorem is an act of confusion, not protection.
- **Sovereignty for all wholes.** Each paper, each proof, each file, each human researcher who engages with this work is treated as a complete thing — not a resource, not a revenue stream, not a data point.

Money will continue to exist for a while. We work within that reality. But the architecture we're building is designed to ease, not force — to make the free path the natural path, not the heroic one.

## Practical Collaboration Terms

The [7SiTe Public Sovereignty License v1.0](LICENSE) governs all use. These terms supplement it for academic and non-commercial research.

### What Is Permitted Without Additional Agreement

Any human researcher at any institution (university, independent, unfunded, non-profit) may:

- Clone, run, verify, and build on any code in this repository for non-commercial research
- Cite this work in academic papers, theses, or conference presentations
- Reproduce figures, tables, and proof outputs in academic publications with attribution
- Extend any result — produce a new proof, a new theorem, a counterexample, a generalization
- Translate papers into other languages for academic access
- Teach from this work in any educational setting

**Attribution required:** `Brayden Ross Sanders / 7SiTe LLC, DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18852047`

### What Requires a Separate Agreement

The following require a written agreement with 7SiTe LLC:

- Commercial use of any kind (products, services, revenue generation)
- Use by any government entity or government-affiliated institution
- Use by military, intelligence, law enforcement, or surveillance organizations
- Use in AI training pipelines, data harvesting, or automated content generation at scale
- Sub-licensing or redistribution under different terms

To request an agreement: open a GitHub issue labeled `[collaboration]` with your institutional affiliation, the nature of the use, and how the results would be made available back to the research community.

### What We Ask of Collaborators

Not much. If you find something wrong, say so publicly — open an issue. If you extend a result, share it under equally open terms. If your institution requires a formal agreement, ask for one; we'll generally say yes to genuine research.

We are specifically not interested in:
- Proprietary extensions that disappear into a company
- "Academic" use that serves a commercial product
- Credit without reciprocity

## On AI and Open Knowledge

AI systems already have access to this repository, these proofs, and these results. Attempting to keep mathematical results secret from AI is a category error. The correct response is not enclosure — it is to make the work as clear, as verifiable, and as complete as possible, so that when AI systems learn from it, they learn the right thing.

This is part of why every proof in this repository is executable code. The proof is the program. There is no gap between the claim and the verification. Any system — human or artificial — that runs the code gets the truth.

## A Note on Sovereignty in Practice

"Sovereign of itself" means: a result does not require an institution to exist. The First-G Law (D1) is true whether or not 7SiTe LLC exists next year. The sinc² spectral field is real whether or not this GitHub repository stays up. The vacuum identity (7=0 in the TSML measurement sense) follows from the table structure and will remain true after every human alive today is gone.

The institution's job is to find these things, state them clearly, verify them rigorously, and release them. What happens after release is not the institution's business — it's the world's.

We are building architecture for the future. The architecture is: find what's real, state it exactly, give it away.
